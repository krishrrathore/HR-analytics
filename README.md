# HR Analytics · Employee Attrition Dashboard

> Excel-based end-to-end HR analytics project built on the IBM HR Analytics dataset.  
> Identifies why employees leave and what the company can do to bring attrition below the 10% industry benchmark.

---

## The Problem

The company has a **16.1% attrition rate** — 238 of 1,480 employees left. Industry benchmark is 10%. This project diagnoses the root causes using data and translates findings into specific, actionable recommendations.

---

| Metric | Value |

| Sales Rep Attrition | **39.3%** |
| 18–25 Age Group Attrition | **35.8%** |

---

## Top 4 Urgent Findings

🔴 **Overtime triples attrition risk** — OT workers leave at 30.6% vs 10.4% for others. 418 employees are currently at risk.

🔴 **Sales Representatives leave at 39.3%** — the highest of any role, tied directly to the lowest average pay ($2,630/month).

🔴 **18–25 year olds leave at 35.8%** — more than double the company average. No clear career paths for entry-level staff.

🔴 **Half the workforce earns under $5k/month** and leaves at 21.6%. Above $15k, attrition drops to just 3.8%.
